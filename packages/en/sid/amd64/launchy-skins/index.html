more skins for launchy, the smart search launcher

Launchy is the most efficient way to open files, folders, websites, and programs on your computer.

These are the non-default skins included with the main distribution:

Note, Spotlight Wide, Simple, Black Glass, Black Glass Wide, Mercury, Mercury Wide and QuickSilver2.

You probably need a compositing window manager for these skins to work properly.
